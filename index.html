    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to common questions about our services, valuation process, and how we help you make informed property decisions

    What is a property valuation, and why is it important?

    A property valuation is a professional assessment of a property's market value. It considers factors such as location, condition, market trends, and comparable sales. Accurate valuations are essential for buying, selling, and financing properties, as well as for legal purposes and investment decisions.

    What factors influence the value of a property?

    A: Key factors include the property’s location, size, condition, and age, along with recent sales of comparable properties. Market trends, zoning, and development potential can also impact value, as can broader economic conditions and interest rates.

    How long does a property valuation take?

    A: The time required varies based on the property’s size, complexity, and location, but a standard valuation typically takes between 1-3 days to complete. Larger or more complex properties may require additional time for a comprehensive assessment.

    What’s the difference between a market value and an investment value?

    Market value represents what a typical buyer would pay for a property under current market conditions, while investment value is the value specific to an individual investor based on their unique needs, objectives, and investment criteria.

    Do you offer consulting services for property investments?

    A: Yes, we provide consulting services to help clients make informed property investment decisions. Our advisors assess market conditions, property potential, and risk factors to guide you through every stage of the investment process.

    Can I use your valuation report for legal or financial purposes?

    Yes, our valuation reports are prepared according to professional standards and can be used for various purposes, including legal proceedings, mortgage applications, taxation, and insurance. We ensure our reports meet all necessary requirements for these uses.

    How much do property valuation services cost?

    A: Our fees vary based on property type, size, and location. We offer competitive rates and transparent pricing. Contact us for a detailed quote tailored to your specific property valuation needs.
